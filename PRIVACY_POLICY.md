# Privacy Policy for PreciosAI

**Last updated: March 14, 2026**

**App version: 1.0.0**

PreciosAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PreciosAI mobile application ("Application").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Our Application is designed with privacy in mind. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.**

### 1.1 Camera Access
The Application requires access to your device's camera to perform real-time object detection and pose recognition. 
- **On-Device Processing**: All image analysis is performed locally on your device. 
- **No Cloud Uploads**: Live camera frames used for AI analysis are processed in volatile memory and are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud services.

### 1.2 Media and Gallery Access
The Application requires access to your device's storage and photo gallery to:
- Save photos captured during your sessions.
- Allow you to select reference images.
- Manage your captured media within the integrated gallery.
- 
All media remains on your device.

### 1.3 Local Storage
We use local storage (via Shared Preferences) to store your app settings, such as similarity thresholds and capture configurations. This data never leaves your device.

## 2. Device Permissions
To provide its core features, the Application explicitly requests the following permissions:
* **CAMERA**: To capture images for real-time AI processing.
* **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE**: To access and select images from your
  gallery on Android 12 and below. On Android 13 and above, the app
  uses the system photo picker, which does not require this permission.
* **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE**: To save processed photos to your device
  on Android 9 and below. On Android 10 and above, no storage permission
  is required to save photos.
* **INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: Used solely to load custom
  fonts (Google Fonts) for the app's interface. No user data is
  transmitted over the internet.

We do not request access to your location, contacts, microphone, or any other sensitive device data.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services
* **No Data Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.
* **No Third-Party Trackers**: The Application does not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase), advertising networks, or tracking SDKs that collect information about your usage.
* **Technical Connectivity**: The Application may use internet access solely for technical purposes, such as loading external assets. No personal data or user-generated media is transmitted over these connections.

## 4. Data Deletion and Retention
Since PreciosAI does not collect or store any user data on external servers, there is no personal data for us to delete.
* **User Control**: You have full control over your data. All images and settings are stored locally. You can delete saved media through the app's gallery or your device’s file manager.
* **App Uninstallation**: If you uninstall the Application, all locally stored app settings and temporary data will be automatically removed by the operating system.

## 5. Security
We value your trust in providing us access to your camera and media. Since all data is processed locally, your photos and analysis results remain under your physical control. We recommend using standard device security (like passcodes or biometrics) to protect your saved media.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from any user, we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes are effective immediately upon posting. We will notify users of significant updates through an in-app notification when feasible.
